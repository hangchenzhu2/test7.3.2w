Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Weather Alert

How accurate are Weather Alert forecasts?

Our weather forecasts use advanced meteorological models with 85-90% accuracy for 3-day forecasts and 70-75% accuracy for 7-day forecasts.

How often is weather data updated?

Current conditions update every 15-30 minutes, hourly forecasts update every hour, and severe weather alerts are issued immediately.

What types of weather alerts do you provide?

We provide tornado warnings, severe thunderstorm alerts, winter storm warnings, hurricane updates, flood alerts, and temperature extreme advisories.

Is Weather Alert free to use?

Yes! Weather Alert is completely free. We believe accurate weather information should be available to everyone for public safety.

How do I set up weather alerts for my location?

Allow location access or enter your city manually, then enable notifications in the settings menu. Your browser will request permission to send alerts.

What do the different weather icons mean?

Our weather icons represent different conditions:

  • Sunny: Clear skies, minimal cloud cover
  • Partly Cloudy: Some clouds, mostly sunny
  • Cloudy: Overcast skies, limited sunshine
  • Rainy: Precipitation expected
  • Thunderstorms: Lightning and thunder possible
  • Snow: Snow or winter precipitation
  • Fog/Mist: Reduced visibility conditions
How do I interpret the air quality index?

The Air Quality Index (AQI) measures air pollution levels:

  • 0-50 (Good): Air quality is satisfactory
  • 51-100 (Moderate): Acceptable for most people
  • 101-150 (Unhealthy for Sensitive Groups): Sensitive individuals may experience symptoms
  • 151-200 (Unhealthy): Everyone may experience health effects
  • 201-300 (Very Unhealthy): Health warnings for everyone
  • 301+ (Hazardous): Emergency conditions, everyone should avoid outdoor activities
Can I use Weather Alert on mobile devices?

Absolutely! Our responsive design works seamlessly on mobile phones, tablets, desktops, and laptops. You can add it to your home screen like a native app.

Why might my location not be detected automatically?

Location detection issues can occur for several reasons:

  • Browser Settings: Location services may be disabled in your browser
  • Privacy Settings: You may have blocked location access for this website
  • Network Issues: Slow or unstable internet connection
  • Device Settings: Location services disabled on your device

If automatic detection fails, you can always manually enter your city name in the search box.

How can I report incorrect weather information?

If you notice incorrect weather information, please help us improve by:

  • Visiting our Contact Support page
  • Including your exact location and the specific issue
  • Providing the time and date when you noticed the discrepancy
  • Comparing with your local observations if possible

We take accuracy seriously and investigate all reports to continuously improve our service.
